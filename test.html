We Bet You Can’t Spell the Top 50 Misspelled Words Correctly

Remember grade school spelling tests? Do you watch the Scripps National Spelling Bee on ESPN like the sport it rightfully is? Okay, maybe people who think spelling’s a sport also think chess is a sport (which it’s not),but if you did really well in school when it came to spelling, we’re willing to wager that you probably wanted a varsity letterman’s jacket to reflect your hard work and brain power. Well, we hope you’ll consider this quiz a retroactive substitute. Can you spell these top 50 misspelled words?

These 50 words aren’t too complicated. In fact, you probably recite them every day, write them down, or type them out. But, does your phone auto-correct your spelling errors before you can edit yourself? Does that squiggly red line shame you so aggressively that you’ve given up on spelling all together? Be warned, however: this quiz looks and maybe feels deceptively easy. Trust that it’s far from that. We’re betting if you’re coming into this quiz super confident, you’re going to get 15 more wrong than you think you will. But hey, you’re welcome to prove us wrong!
